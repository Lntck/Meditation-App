Find serenity in every breath with Inner Breeze.
This app guides you through the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wim_Hof">Wim Hof Breathing Method</a> with ease.
Experience mindful breathing with personalized settings and track your journey towards tranquility and rejuvenation.

This easy yet potent breathing technique promises profound inner peace,
offering a sanctuary of serenity amidst life's hectic pace.

Simply follow 4 steps:
<ol>
<li>Inhale and exhale rhythmically for 30 breaths.</li>
<li>Exhale and hold your breath.</li>
<li>Inhale deeply and hold for 15 seconds.</li>
<li>Exhale and repeat step 1.</li>
</ol>

Do 3-10 rounds of the process.
